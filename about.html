About Us

Our Mission

Medicinal plants which deliver us with leaves, flowers, fruits, nuts, and roots are amazing gifts from Mother Earth. It's our exciting exploration to find out how abundantly they nurture us on our daily life and provide us vitality. We make products from those parts of the plants to maintain our good health in the most natural way

Our Values

We had served over 24,000 client visits with the alternative treatment providing herbal medicine and acupuncture for over 15 years. We deeply acknowledge how powerful medicinal plants can heal for human well-being. More than 60% of the world relies on the herbal medicinal treatment. Specifically, this traditional treatment method is based on the oriental philosophy which is very popular in South Asia with its proven benefits. We keep on applying herbs complementing nutritional foods to optimize our health for a holistically happy life.

Our Vision

Canadian ginseng has the best quality thanks to the friendly-ecological environment, unique climate, ideal soil, clean water along with the sustainable agriculture practices in Canada. These features bring back the high-quality ginseng rich in nutrients with a distinctive taste and flavor. We'd love to bring this premium ginseng to enhance for our optimal health with a variety of good products for many choices. We desire to popularize Canadian ginseng to be recognized as the best quality ginseng in the world.
